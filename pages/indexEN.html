Vision of the Platform

Et Exchange is the first distributed exchange in the world to advocate complete decentralization, hoping to establish an efficient and transparent asset trading platform to meet the spirit of block chain and serve all global digital asset projects and digital currency asset users, promoting the progress and development of the block chain industry.

Bancor

The emergence of EOS RAM successfully verified the feasibility of realizing EOS decentralization transactions through bancor protocol. Base on this, we have designed the operation mode of ET trading platform, hoping to solve the pain spots of the existing trading platform by this idea, which will be beneficial to the entire block chain digital asset industry chain.

Advantages

  • Large User Base
  • Complete Transparent and Fair
  • Openness of the Platform
  • EOS Underlying Technologies
  • Profit Model

The ET open trading platform can be accessed using all of the EOS wallets as a portal, and it the perfectly inherit the large user base of EOS system, which supports all transaction between the tokens of EOS chains and EOS, and will supports the cross chain transactions of all currencies in future, such as BTC, ETH, and all currencies to ESDT transactions.

All transactions on the ET trading platform are automatically traded through the intelligent contract bancor algorithm. All data is published in real time and all information is completely symmetric and transparent. Users can query the data on the chain in real time through the block browser, avoiding a series of trust problems caused by opacity.

ET open trading platform will be the first trading platform in the world who advocates the openness and self-governing base on the block chain technologies and simplified pass-through concept. All codes are open source, all interfaces are open, all existing trading platforms can seamlessly connect and enjoy the profits generated by exchanges on the platforms.

The DPOS mechanism of EOS has passed the digital high frequency test, which can carry a large number of concurrency, cope with the rapidly increasing demand for orders. Trading system will adopt distributed parallel technologies, which can be expanded at any time with the increase of transaction volume. When the traffic is pouring in, the load balance will be split according to different services, which can make the business to be expanded independently, in addition, The cluster built by the cloud server can also achieve hot expansion.

The ET trading platform can provide all the tokens based on EOS in the early stage, and even the trading pairs of all currencies in the block chain world can be provided in the later stage. The demand is huge, and the exchange fees incurred will be very impressive.
